FAQ

What is Fenix?

Fenix is the first fully-featured, cloud-based, high-end computer. It is the only remote service that offers performance capable of competing with a local PC.

On what type of devices is Fenix available?

Fenix works with nearly any device that has an internet connection. At the moment, there are Windows, macOS, Android, Android TV, iOS, tvOS, and Linux apps available. Please check out our requirements tab to know If Shadow would work with your device.

Will I be able to play on Fenix right after I subscribe?

Depending on the demand, your access time may vary. During your purchase your ''ready to use'' date will be displayed all along and will also be visible on your account page.
